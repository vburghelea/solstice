## Potential Talking Points

### Why In-App Training?

- Users learn in context, not in separate training sessions
- Always up-to-date (no stale PDF guides)
- Self-paced - users learn when they need to
- Reduces support burden - answers are findable

### Scalable Onboarding

- 60 PSOs can onboard themselves via walkthroughs
- viaSport doesn't have to manually train each org
- Templates ensure consistent data format
