# Open Decisions and Missing Inputs

These items require owner decisions or external input before final assembly.

## 1) Branding and visual identity

- Final color palette and typography.
- Whether to mirror viaSport brand colors or use Solstice branding.
- Cover page hero image selection and approval.

## 2) Company name and contact details

- Confirm the legal name to use consistently (Austin Wallace Tech vs Austin Wallace Tech Corporation).
- Confirm the address and contact email to place on the cover.

## 3) Cover letter and authorized contacts

- Whether a formal cover letter should be included.
- Final authorized contact list and titles.

## 4) Team bios and headshots

- Short bios for all advisory partners for Appendix F.
- Decision on including headshots or keeping text only.

## 5) Pricing and commercial terms

- Confirm pricing figures for 3 year and 5 year totals.
- Confirm implementation fee vs subscription split.
- Decide whether optional add-ons or in-kind contributions appear in the main body.

## 6) SLA targets and support model

- Confirm SLA response and resolution targets.
- Decide whether to include credits or penalties language.

## 7) Case studies and references

- Final list of case studies and whether names can be public.
- Decision on including client references or anonymized examples.

## 8) Prototype access details

- Final demo credentials and whether to publish them in the PDF.
- Delivery method for videos (embedded in zip vs hosted links).

## 9) Security and compliance positioning

- Confirm if any external security assessments or pen tests can be referenced.
- Decide whether to include a shared responsibility model diagram.

## 10) Exceptions or contract deviations

- Confirm whether any exceptions or redlines should be listed.

## 11) Delivery timeline assumptions

- Confirm the target go live window and any fixed milestones.
- Confirm expected data freeze duration and parallel run policy.

## 12) AI and automation positioning

- Decide the level of AI emphasis to include.
- Confirm which AI features are implemented vs optional.

## 13) Evidence pack scope

- Decide whether to include a full requirement-by-requirement evidence folder or only the curated evidence pack.
- Decide whether to include raw logs (DR drills, encryption status) in the submission zip.

## 14) Compliance crosswalk format

- Confirm whether to add a Confirmed column and evidence references in the crosswalk.
